SEO (SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMISATION)

  1. Home
  2. SEO (SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMISATION)

Google Ads, previously known as AdWords, is a powerful advertising platform provided by Google. It enables companies to advertise their products or services to specific target audiences across various digital platforms like Google search results, websites, mobile apps, and YouTube.

What is the importance of Google Ads?

Google Ads offers advertisers comprehensive targeting options.

  • Find potential customers based on interests, location, language, and demographics.
  • It enables the display of advertisements to the target audience according to the types of devices suitable for advertising.
  • Allows you to change the budget and targeting options of advertising campaigns at any time.
  • From the moment the advertisements are published, they can reach potential customers and reach your target audience.
  • It is easy for you to determine the advertising budget and pay according to the impressions and clicks of the advertisements.

SEO is a set of technical and strategic applications that enable a website to appear organically at the top of search engines.

What is the importance of SEO in short?

  • It ensures that your website ranks high in search engines.
  • You get the chance to reach potential customers directly in search engines.
  • It allows you to attract organic customer traffic to your website.
  • It allows you to stay ahead of your competitors.
  • Increases the conversion rates of your website, increases the potential for more sales.
